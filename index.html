About Me

Good Day! My name is Norm Miller! I am a junior majoring in Data Science who is also pursuing an academic minor in history. Last semester, I took part in the Honors Scholars program where I did an honors credit in a regular for my ENG 2142 poetry class. In that project, I used Google Colaboratory, using data mining, visualization, management, and analyzation techniques to showcase different genres and eras of poetry. I am also a member of the New York Public Interest Research Group (NYPIRG) at City Tech, where I am one of our student statewide delegate for our campus. We come up with proposals to help with the public interest of New Yorkers across the state. So if you want to learn more about me, please, don't hesitate to contact me.
